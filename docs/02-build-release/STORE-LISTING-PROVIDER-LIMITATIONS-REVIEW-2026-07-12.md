# Store Listing Provider Limitations Review

Reviewed: 2026-07-12, Australia/Sydney
Reviewer: Leo Kesselring

## Approved disclosure copy

Fuel Path shows the fuel-price source and freshness for each result. Prices can
change after they are displayed, so drivers should confirm the price before
driving or buying fuel. Recommendations account for route and eligibility
settings, but do not guarantee a saving, station availability or statewide
coverage in every circumstance.

Fuel Path does not claim prediction accuracy or that alerts will be sent when
a provider, freshness, route, device-permission or delivery gate prevents it.

## Review outcome

- The copy is consistent with current in-app source/freshness evidence and
  conservative recommendation language.
- It contains no unsupported universal live-price, guaranteed-saving or
  prediction-accuracy claim.
- It must be included in the final App Store and Google Play listing materials
  before submission.

This is reviewed listing-disclosure evidence only. It does not prove that a
public App Store or Google Play listing exists or has been approved.
